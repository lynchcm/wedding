ACCOMMODATION

There are lots of hotels and B&B's within walking distance of the venue. These tend to book up very quickly so please book early!

GETTING THERE

By Car

Use postcode SK17 9AR or put ‘pavilion gardens car park’ in to google maps. There is a 270-space pay-and-display car park on the corner of St John's Road and Burlington Road; 2–4 hours costs £3.40, over 4 hours is £5.20. Parking charges apply 8am to 6pm even on Sunday, so if you leave your car overnight you'll need to move it by 8am. If you plan to drive and would be willing to lift share, let us know!

By Train

The winter garden conservatory is a six-minute walk from Buxton train station. From London Euston, it’s just over three hours with one change at Stockport, and tickets cost around £50.

By Plane

Manchester is the nearest airport. A return train ticket from Manchester airport costs £25–30 if you book in advance, but there's also the Skyline 199 coach which costs £5.50 each way (no need to book in advance).
